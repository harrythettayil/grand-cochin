About Us

Welcome to Grand Cochin, Chelmsford’s home for authentic Kerala cuisine. We bring you time-honored recipes, rich spices, and coastal flavors straight from God’s Own Country.

From fluffy appams to flavorful Malabar biryani, every dish is crafted with tradition and passion. Join us for a taste of Kerala, right here in Chelmsford.
